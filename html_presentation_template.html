Workflow

How to present on an extended screen

  1. Open this HTML file in Chrome.
  2. Drag the window to the projector or second monitor.
  3. Press F for fullscreen on that screen.
  4. Press P to open presenter view on your laptop.
  5. Use arrow keys or a clicker to move through slides.

Tip: You can duplicate this file and create one deck per talk. Replace the sample slides with your own content or embed your local HTML dashboards.
